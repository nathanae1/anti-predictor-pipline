About Equality

Equality is a project born from an understanding that being committed to an idea requires action. The issues of gender and racial bias are finally getting the media attention they deserve, but there are still areas that need improvement.

Computer models have emerged as an easy way to counter biases, and can be more fair than humans are. Unfortunately, they can also be just as biased as humans are, with the unfortunate side effect of being difficult to interpret (it's easier to tell when a human is discriminating).

So I created Equality, both a way to test how much a model is predicting on a variable not in it (such as gender or race) and a tool to enable you to remove those biases from any model.
